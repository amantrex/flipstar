Find the Star

How to Play

  • Flip tiles to find the hidden star.
  • You have 10 flips and 30 seconds to find it.
  • Each tile flip reveals a hint showing the distance to the star.
  • Green tiles also flip their adjacent top, bottom, left, and right tiles.
  • Blue tiles also flip their diagonal neighbors.
  • Use logic and hints to narrow down the star's location.
  • Click "Play Again" to restart the game.
  • Good luck!
